# Image brief — real smiling-people photos to replace generic stock

Goal: swap the generic dental-tool / equipment hero images for **real, relatable, smiling people** so the brand feels warm and approachable.

## Specs (apply to every photo)
- **Orientation:** portrait, **4:5 ratio** ideal (e.g. 1200 × 1500 px). Landscape is OK but will be cropped to 4:5 (subject centred).
- **Resolution:** min ~1000 px wide, ideally 1500 px+.
- **Style:** natural light, **genuine smile (teeth showing is great for a dental brand)**, muted / neutral background, real-looking people — not glossy models. Matches the site's calm, editorial, muted palette.
- **Diversity:** mix of ages and ethnicities (Inner West Sydney is multicultural).
- **AHPRA:** these are **decorative/lifestyle** images only — they must NOT be presented as patients or treatment results (the real before/after sections are separate and stay as-is). If any are **real patients**, we need **signed consent** for advertising use.
- **Delivery:** send files or links and say which slot each goes to → I re-host on Cloudinary (optimised) and wire them in.
